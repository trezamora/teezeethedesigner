About Me

ALOHA! I'm Tre Zamora, a Freelance Graphic Designer based in Honolulu, HI that specialize in Visual Identity, UX/UI, Type, and Print Design.

I've been a design hobbyist since 2015 before officially making it my career in 2021. Having worked with clients both locally and all the way on the east coast ranging in a vast array of industries including; education, finance, fitness, food & beverage, sociology, and marketing.

I hope to shed and share my perspective on design with the world one solution at a time, starting with you!
