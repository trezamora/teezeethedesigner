Art For You

Inspire your art magazine with the past.

Honoring the history of art and design through typesetting.

Project Overview

Design an honorable magazine combining elements from historical eras with contemporary influences from designers and artists.

Services

Print Design & Typesetting

Software

Adobe Creative Cloud: Illustrator, PhotoShop, XD, Social Media

Challenge

Design a magazine to look appealing, provide information to the reader, and be influenced by historical design principles.

Solution

Research findings from various sources can be used to influence the style of designs and typesettings.

Conceptual Planning

The idea behind "Art For You" magazine is to celebrate the past of art and design, with a special focus on historic times. It outlines the magazine's goals, such as educating readers about historic periods, showcasing contemporary designs, and inspiring a passion for and appreciation for them.

Into the Past

The design of the magazine was inspired by the rich history of art and design, and our aim was to honor the designers and artists who have made significant contributions to the field. I wanted the magazine to reflect the evolution of design, from traditional techniques to contemporary practices, and to demonstrate how the past has shaped and influenced the present.

Research

Start with some research about the Bauhaus era and how it affected design and art. Using this research, you can get ideas for the magazine's overall design and content. Look at the work of famous Bauhaus designers and artists, as well as contemporary designers influenced by the movement.

Concept

Research and define the concept for the magazine. The goal is to create a publication that promotes an appreciation of the Bauhaus era, celebrating the past of art and design

Audience

Consider who the magazine is for. Is it for design enthusiasts, artists, or students? Knowing the target audience helps determine the tone, content, and design.

Content

Determine the content for each issue based on the concept and target audience. This might include an article or two about Bauhaus artists and designers, an interview with a contemporary designer, and a show of current designs.

Layout

Begin designing the magazine's layout based on the research and content planning performed, using elements inspired by the Bauhaus era and a focus on typography.

Typesetting

Make sure the typesetting for the magazine is in keeping with the overall design, inspired by the Bauhaus period.

Production

Examine the design and content of the magazine, making any necessary revisions to ensure that the magazine is visually appealing, informative, and aligned with the target audience,concept, and them produce.

Thankful Teachings

Creating a magazine that combines elements of eras history and design, with a focus on typography, as a tribute to the past of art and design. The magazine will serve as a reminder of the significance of this style and encourage readers to learn and appreciate its history.
